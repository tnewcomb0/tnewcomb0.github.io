Doubles

Doubles is a mobile application that helps users find their next athletic partner. Just like Tinder, you can review profiles and swipe left or right anonymously, and begin in-app conversations with your matches without having to exchange your phone number or email. Doubles was built using full-stack Ruby on Rails, with a handful of ReactJS components layered on to the front-end to make for a more seamless user experience. It was built over the course of one week as our team's final project at Dev Bootcamp. I was in charge of the UI/UX and the overall site styling and design.
